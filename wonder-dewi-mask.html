FAQs

  1. Q1 How often should I use face sheet mask?

    Every healthy skin care regimen includes daily use and weekly use products. You can use a face mask once a week, or you can use it more then that depending on your skin and your skin care concerns. Many recommend using it three times a week or once a day for extra care. Wonderlab brought you WonderDewi set, from 7 days, 14 days and 28days, check out the schedule that we have prepared, and learn more about treating your skin with a sheet mask!

  2. Q2 Can I use face sheet mask everyday?

    Using a sheet mask every day can help boost your skin's hydration levels, as leaving the mask on the face for a certain period helps push the ingredients into the skin, you can enjoy the same result as using a super-powered moisturizer every day.

  3. Q3 should I wash my face after sheet mask?

    There is no need to wash your face after using a sheet mask. If there is serum left on your skin after using the mask, gently pat the serum into your skin until it is fully absorbed. You can apply moisturizer to your skin after using a sheet mask, it gives you double treat by sealing the moist in your skin.

  4. Q4 Are sheet masks good for skin?

    Sheet masks are an easy and the most cost-effective way to provide your skin with an immediate and big boost of vitamins and moisture. They're also the most convenient method to relax and pamper yourself at home!

  5. Q5 Which comes first: toner or mask?

    Apply toner before face mask. All treatment masks work best when applied to skin that has been prepped properly. Moreover, toner can help soothe irritated skin, rid the skin of excess impurities and oil, to prevent premature aging.

  6. Q6 Is it okay to leave sheet mask overnight?

    Remember: do not leave a sheet mask overnight! Sheet masking should be kept between 15-20 minutes and should not exceed 30 minutes. Leaving sheet masks on until they dry will cause a reversal effect because the sheet will begin to reabsorb the moisture from your skin.

  7. Q7 Where do you store sheet masks?

    Keep your sheet masks at room temperature for a more calming experience. The coldness of a chilled sheet mask from the fridge tightens your pores, but it can also wake you up. If you like to do your sheet masks before bed, keep them stored at room temperature so that they aren't shocking when you put them on.

  8. Q8 Can I reuse sheet masks?

    You should not reuse the same sheet mask. Yes, there's usually a ton of excess serum at the bottom of sheet-mask packets, but the last thing you want to do is reintroduce the dirt and bacteria from your face by re-soaking and reusing the same mask. You can apply the excess serum on your neck or elbow!

  9. Q9 Do sheet masks expired?

    Yes, sheet masks do expire. Do check the expiry date on the package.

  10. Q10 Can I use expired face sheet mask?

    Besides less effectiveness, the danger with expired masks may lead to skin irritation and possible bacterial infection, as the chemicals and ingredients in the essence begin to break down.

    As our mask didnt contain any aggresive ingredient, it suitable for any skin type. For super sensitive skin and acne problem, will adsive to cut the first time usage to 5-8mins instead of 15mins. If there is not any irritation reaction, sensitive and acne skin can add on the mask time to 10mins or 15mins.
